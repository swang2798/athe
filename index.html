About

Just a cozy spot for coffee

Hey! I'm running everydayathe from my home. I love making coffee and wanted to share that with people in a chill, relaxed way.

I make everything fresh and keep it simple. Good coffee, some snacks, and a friendly place to hang out. Nothing fancy, just good vibes.

Come by if you want a cup of coffee and a chat. It's pretty casual around here!
